The Muse Project


The Muse project(formerly known as Eden) was concieved out of the need for the Walmart Global Supply Chain to treat organic products differently than nonorganic products from a supply chain perspective.

To that end Muse represents a platforms of desktop and moblie applicagions aimed at predicting and observing produce quality.
Core Transcription and Translation API


Authenticity.ai was founded to provide highly accurate Artificial Intelligence surveillance solutions to the Legal and Compliance industries.

Authenticity provides audio and video recognition solutions with custom trainable artificial intelligence engines for audio transcription, language translation, facial recognition and object detection.
Curriculum Management Solutions for High Education


Curricunet, formerly Governet provides a curriculum management solution for colleges and instiatutions of higher learning needing a simple solution for managing change within their curriculums.

The tool provides a framework which makes getting new courses and programs approved by accrediting bodies much easier and straight forward.
ConformX and Solex Esigning solution


Docutech provides a several soltions that together faciliate the entire loan approval process electronically. Loan Service orginators can submit borrower information and get graunteed valid electronic or paper documents needed to close a loan.

ConformX(The main document generating platform) and Solex(The mobile signing solution) along with several other services provide the basis for an entirely paperless end to end mortgage solution from Initial Disclosure to post closing.
